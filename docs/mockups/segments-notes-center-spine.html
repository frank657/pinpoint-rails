How it reads: the ember segment span sits on the spine; the 0:36 note’s dot lands right on it, so containment is obvious — while 0:00 (intro) and the 0:27–0:39 range clearly sit outside/across it. Progressive disclosure: collapsed cards show just time + title; hovering reveals the description, tags, and the segment’s exact range — keeping a long panel scannable. Behaviors to add: hovering a segment dims notes outside its span (and vice-versa); click anything to seek. Same derived rule as before — a note is “in” a segment when segment.start ≤ note.start < segment.end; no schema change.
